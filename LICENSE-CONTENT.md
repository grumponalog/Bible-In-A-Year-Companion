## A note on the content itself

The descriptions summarize widely held traditional and academic/critical positions
on the authorship, dating, and historical setting of the biblical books. Dates are
scholarly approximations and are debated. The content is provided as a study aid and
makes no claim to settle contested scholarly questions.
