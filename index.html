How to use this: Books are arranged chronologically by when the events occurred, not by the usual page order — so Job sits beside Genesis, and the prophets are slotted into the kingdom years they spoke into. For each book you get two columns: the traditional attribution held in Jewish & Christian tradition, and the academic/critical view of modern scholarship. Where they agree, the columns will look alike; where they diverge (as with Genesis or Daniel), you'll see both. The two long sections below those columns give you the deep background — the world of the writing, and the world of the events.
